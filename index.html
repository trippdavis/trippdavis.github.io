Asteroids

Hit Space to Start Game

Instructions:

You get points by shooting asteroids. Getting hit by an asteroid will result in losing a life. Your ship is equipped with three different guns for you to use: a pistol which shoots a single bullet, a shotgun which shoots 5 bullets, and a laser which shoots a single laser beam. Bullets will be destroyed after hitting an asteroid while lasers will survive and can destroy multiple asteroids. You have limited ammo for each gun, but ammo refills randomly appear as small circles: green for pistol, red for shotgun, and blue for laser. Enjoy!
